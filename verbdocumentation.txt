Verb Categories in the original doc
0:
    Declerative verb. Initiales the container
    eg: Shivansh has 10 apples.
t+:
    A positive transfer involving two people.
    eg: Suyash bought 10 apples from Tejasvi.
+:
    A positive change involving only 1 person.
    eg: I grew by 6 feet.
t-:
    A negative transfer involving two people.
    eg: Tejasvi gave Suyash 10 pens.
-:
    A negative change involving only 1 person.
    eg: Shivansh lost 2 pens on his way.
