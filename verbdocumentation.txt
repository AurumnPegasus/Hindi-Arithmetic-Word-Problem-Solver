Verb Categories in the original doc
0:
    used to define the number of things possessed by the person
    I have 10 coins
t+:
    Something that is acquired(addition) from someone else(transfer)
    I bought 10 pens from Shivansh.
+:
    being used as a verb for Something increasing(addition) in quantity
    I grew by 6 feet.
++:
    being used to combine smaller parts together to form a bigger object
    eg-build a house
t-:
    being used as a verb of giving(subtraction) Something to someone else(transfer)
    eg-feed
-:
    being used as a verb of splitting into parts/breaking up
    eg-cracked
--:
    being used as a verb for being depleted/finished
